About

Professional Background

Mardoqueo Arteaga is an applied economist at LinkedIn, where he provides marketing science and insights support to major enterprise clients. The work relies on LinkedIn's unique data and point of view to tell stories with data and conduct Sales research with econometrics.

Before joining LinkedIn, he was on the Economic and Statistical Consulting group at KPMG, where he served as a project lead to clients in healthcare, telecommunications, state agencies, financial services, and transfer pricing. His work spanned causal inference for labor programs, probabilistic simulation for complex assets, and research on policy changes.

Mardoqueo received his PhD in Economics, with a specialty in belief formation, from Fordham University and previously worked as a visiting associate researcher at the Central Bank of Chile.
