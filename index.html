Hello!

I am a PhD Candidate in Economics and the Vice President of the graduate student government at Fordham University. I am also a visiting PhD student at Columbia University and a visiting researcher at the Central Bank of Chile. My interests focus on the role of beliefs and expectations in macroeconomics, policy, and finance.

A note to students from underrepresented backgrounds: please reach out if you wish to talk about professional development or paths to higher education. I share my experiences as an immigrant from El Salvador and DACA holder here.

All views are my own, not of any of my employers.
