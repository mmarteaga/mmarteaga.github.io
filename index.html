Biography

Mardoqueo (Marc) is an Economist at LinkedIn. His work bridges data science, economics, and behavioral insights to deliver data-driven marketing strategy and client-focused storytelling.

He holds a doctorate in economics, specialty in belief formation, from Fordham University; his thesis was a series of empirical studies on how macroeconomic expectations are formed and turn into behavior. His background spans policy creation, statistical consulting, and analytics, enabling a holistic approach to business problem-solving and communication.

The views expressed on this site are my own, and do not reflect those of any of my former or current employers.
