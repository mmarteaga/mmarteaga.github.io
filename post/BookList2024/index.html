Book List 2024

Mardoqueo Arteaga
December 31, 2024

Introduction

What a year! Ups and downs, many twists and turns as life often has. But, I am back living in Manhattan which fulfills some hidden desire within me. À mon seul désir. I share my 2024 Book List below, as well as my thoughts in the accompanying PDF.

  • The Price of Salt, Patricia Highsmith (1952)
  • The White Tiger, Aravind Adiga (2009)
  • Bankers in the Ivory Tower, Charlie Eaton (2022)
  • Babel, or the Necessitty of Violence, R. F. Kuang (2022)
  • Mountolive, Lawrence Durell (1958)
  • Cutting for Stone, Abraham Verghese (2009)
  • The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo, Taylor Jenkins Reid (2017)
  • Co-Intelligence, Ethan Mollick (2024)
  • The Wings of the Dove, Henry James (1902)
  • The Monk, Matthew Lewis (1796)
